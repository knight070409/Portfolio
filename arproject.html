AR EdTech Game (under development)

AR EdTeach is an augmented reality educational tool currently in development, aimed at enhancing the learning experience in biology by visualizing DNA structures in 3D. Built using Unity and Vuforia, the application will allow users to interact with and explore DNA molecules in augmented reality. The goal is to demonstrate how mutations in DNA can lead to various genetic disorders, providing a more immersive and intuitive way to understand complex biological concepts.

As the Unity Developer on the project, I am responsible for implementing the AR experience, integrating Vuforia for image tracking, and scripting interactive elements in C#. This project is helping me strengthen my skills in AR development and explore how immersive technology can transform science education.
